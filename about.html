Our Story

Building Excellence Since 1998

Khan Construction was founded in 1998 by Nawaz Khan with a clear vision — to deliver premium construction services that combine modern engineering with uncompromising quality.

Based in Nagpur, Maharashtra, we have successfully completed residential villas, commercial complexes, and infrastructure projects across the region, earning the trust of hundreds of clients.

Our team of experienced civil engineers and construction professionals work together to ensure every project is delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.
